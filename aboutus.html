ABOUT HAMPTON MOTORSPORTS

The one-stop-shop for Motorsports enthusiasts!

Hampton Motorsports is a family-run business and MotorSports Community in the heart of the beautiful Hamptons on the east end of Long Island. Our showroom on the main highway is a welcoming place where friends and riders can stop by for a chat, browse our extensive collection of vehicles, helmets, and accessories, or just hang out! We're always around to answer your questions, schedule appointments, or unfold a map to show you the best routes in the Hamptons. After all, we love all motor and power sports and ride our beach and country roads every chance we get!

We are a factory-authorized dealer for Vespa, Piaggio, Benelli, SSR Motorsports, Apollo, Genuine Scooter Company, Triton Trailers, E-Z-Go, Hyosung, Kymco, Hammerhead Off-Road, Chicago Scooter Co, and Strider. In addition to new units, parts, and accessories, we also curated a selection of pre-owned motorcycles, ATVs, dirt bikes, utility vehicles, scooters, trailers, and generators. We offer full service and inspections, year-round storage, pick-up and delivery, and can even help you with financing and insurance. We want to get you safely and quickly out on the road to enjoy your vehicle.

Our service technicians are factory-trained and certified to be the best. They are highly skilled professionals, dedicated to providing timely and expert service, and committed to excellence. Their knowledge of parts and accessories is second-to-none. In the event you have a unique request, they will access the information from our state-of-the- art database. And while we carry an extensive selection of reasonably priced, genuine factory parts and accessories, what we don't have in stock, we will special order for you!

Come in to check out our huge inventory of accessories by THOR, Moose Racing, Troy Lee Designs, AGV Helmets, HJC Helmets, Vespa, Alpinestars, Renthal, Fly Racing, Icon, NEXX, Spectro, and much, much more. And if you can't decide what to buy, purchase a gift certificate and send the motorsports enthusiast in your life to our showroom.

Our goal is to not only meet your expectations but to exceed them. So, come on in and join the family!
